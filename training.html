About Training Program

Uajala offers a dynamic training program known as the Product Design Workshop, which spans two intensive days of hands-on learning. This workshop is tailored to cater to the needs of the design team, engineers, and managers within the company. Participants engage in a deep dive into the principles of industrial design and product aesthetics, gaining valuable insights and practical skills. The objective of this workshop is to enhance understanding of design fundamentals and foster creativity in product development. Through interactive sessions and collaborative exercises, attendees are equipped with the tools and knowledge necessary to drive innovation and excellence in their respective roles.
